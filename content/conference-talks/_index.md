### Pulse Europe 2025 — Rewriting the Customer Education Content Playbook with Modular Content

At Pulse Europe 2025, I presented on how modular content strategies can transform the way SaaS companies build and scale educational materials. The talk explored how breaking content into reusable, structured components allows teams to deliver more consistent, maintainable, and personalized learning experiences across their customer base.
